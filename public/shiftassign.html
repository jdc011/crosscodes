Shift Assign


Synopsis
This Android app is written for resturant managers and employees. It features shift assignment through a calandar allowing employees to see their shifts and whether shifts can be changed. Eployees and managers can also chat with each other.

Details
Parse is the database server of choice when implementing employee/ manager accounts and storing shift times. All members of local to a resturant will have their shift times mapped to them through parse. There is also a chat client that is implemented using Node.js. Several networking capabilities of this app is implemented through socket.io. This app only works online.
